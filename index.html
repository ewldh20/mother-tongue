Welcome to Mother Tongue

Mother Tongue is an AI-driven language practice tool designed to help you unlock your full language potential through real conversations. While apps like Duolingo are great for learning words, true fluency comes from speaking. Scientifically, regular conversation practice builds neural pathways in your brain, helping you think in the language rather than just memorizing vocabulary.

I created this app because I used to spend a fortune on tutors every week to practice speaking. Now, with Mother Tongue, I’ve developed a super simple, efficient, and much more affordable solution that can be fit around your schedule.

Best Practice for Using Mother Tongue

1
Start the Alexa skill
Say, "Alexa, open Mother Tongue."
2
Choose a language
Alexa will ask, "What language would you like to practice?" Respond with a language, such as “Francais” or “Espanol”.
3
Set the duration
Alexa will ask, "For how long would you like to practice?" Respond with a time in the relevant language.
4
Choose a topic
Alexa will ask, "What topic would you like to discuss?" Respond with a topic in the language you're practicing.
5
Begin speaking!
Speak in the selected language and let Alexa guide the conversation in real-time.

How to Change the Locale on Your Alexa

1
Open the Alexa app
Go to the Alexa app on your smartphone or tablet.
2
Go to Devices
Tap on the Devices icon at the bottom of the screen.
3
Select Echo & Alexa
Choose the Echo & Alexa option from the top menu.
4
Choose your device
Select the Alexa device you're using for Mother Tongue.
5
Scroll to Language
Scroll down and tap on Language.
6
Select the language
Select the language you want to practice, for example, French or Spanish.
