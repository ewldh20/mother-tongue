Getting Started

1
Start the Alexa skill
Say, "Alexa, open Mother Tongue."
2
Choose a language
Alexa will ask, "What language would you like to practice?" Respond with a language, such as “Spanish”.
3
Set the duration
Alexa will ask, "For how long would you like to practice?" Respond with a time such as "5 minutes"
4
Choose a topic
Alexa will ask, "What topic would you like to discuss?" Respond with a topic such as "weekends"
5
Begin speaking!
Speak in the selected language and let Alexa guide the conversation in real-time.

Mobile App

Enjoy the convenience of using Mother Tongue on your mobile device through the Alexa app. Practice languages wherever you are!

Steps to Download the Alexa App on Your Mobile Device:

1
Open the App Store
For iPhone users, open the App Store. For Android users, open the Google Play Store.
2
Search for Amazon Alexa
In the app store's search bar, type Amazon Alexa and tap search.
3
Download the App
Tap the download or install button to get the app on your device.
4
Log in to the Alexa App
Once downloaded, open the Alexa app and log in using your Amazon account.
5
Navigate to Skills & Games
In the Alexa app, tap the More button (three horizontal lines at the bottom-right of the screen) and select Skills & Games.
6
Search for Mother Tongue
In the Skills & Games section, search for Mother Tongue and select it.
7
Enable the Skill
Tap Enable to activate the Mother Tongue skill on your Alexa app.
8
Start Practicing
You can now use Mother Tongue on your mobile device! Simply say, "Alexa, open Mother Tongue" to begin practicing languages on the go.

Using the Alexa app on your phone allows you to practice speaking in different languages wherever you are!
